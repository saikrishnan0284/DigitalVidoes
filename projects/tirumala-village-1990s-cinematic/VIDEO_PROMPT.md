# Tirumala Village 1990s Cinematic Video - Production Prompt

**Project:** Rural Andhra Pradesh 1990s Nostalgia  
**Duration:** 2 minutes  
**Setting:** Village near Tirumala 7 Hills, Early 1990s  
**Style:** Ultra-realistic cinematic documentary

---

## Project Overview

Create a highly realistic 2-minute cinematic video set in rural Andhra Pradesh during the early 1990s, with the sacred Tirumala 7 hills visible from a distant village. The atmosphere should feel nostalgic, peaceful, authentic, and culturally accurate to that era.

---

## Scene & Environment

### Opening Shot
The video opens with a wide sunrise shot of the Tirumala seven hills visible far away from a countryside village.

### Village Authenticity
The village must authentically represent a 1990s South Indian rural setting.

### Prohibited Modern Elements
**No modern elements should appear:**
- No smartphones
- No modern vehicles
- No concrete apartments
- No LED boards
- No modern clothing styles
- No electric poles with modern wiring styles

### Infrastructure
- Roads should be narrow mud roads and partially stone roads from the 1990s village era
- Houses should be traditional old-style Andhra village homes:
  - Clay tile roofs
  - Lime-coated walls
  - Wooden doors
  - Open verandas
  - Small courtyards

### Natural Environment
Include very large native village trees:
- Banyan trees
- Neem trees
- Tamarind trees
- Trees should look decades old and naturally grown

---

## Village Life Details

### Authentic Rural Activities (1990s)

**Children playing in dusty village streets:**
- Marbles
- Spinning tops
- Running barefoot
- Cricket with wooden bats

**Community Life:**
- Old men sitting together under a tree discussing daily village matters
- Women drawing water, sweeping front yards, and talking near houses
- Farmers working in green agricultural fields

**Animals & Nature:**
- Buffaloes walking slowly through the streets and near ponds
- Birds flying naturally across the village skies:
  - Parrots
  - Sparrows
  - Crows
  - Pigeons

**Traditional Elements:**
- Bullock carts moving slowly on village roads
- Smoke from traditional wood-fired kitchens rising softly in the morning

### Visual Quality

Natural ambient sounds:
- Birds chirping

### Camera Movement
Slow cinematic camera movements:
- Drone wide shots
- Tracking village streets
- Close-up emotional shots

## Camera Sequence Suggestions

1. Sunrise over Tirumala hills from far village
2. Slow pan across fields and buffaloes
3. Children playing in village streets
4. Farmers in agriculture fields
5. Old men discussion under banyan tree
6. Women working near homes
7. Birds flying across giant trees
8. Evening golden-hour village life
9. Final sunset shot with Tirumala hills in background
